About Me

I've always loved strolling around...

I was the kid always somewhere else in her head. ADHD, they'd say. Dreamer, I prefer.

My favorite thing has always been losing myself in a city, an album in my ears, no agenda, just walking.

When I arrived in Lisbon, I didn't explore it. I strolled it. Every single day.

Years earlier, my dad had to pick five albums titles that sounded like me for a school project. First on his list: Strollin' by Chet Baker. He knew before I did.

Food has always been at the center of everything for me, growing up in a family where meals were not just eaten, they were celebrated. That obsession led me to work with restaurants in Brussels, chase flavors through Paris, and eventually land in Lisbon, which completely stole my heart.

Somewhere along the way, I discovered I love making videos — not the polished, overproduced kind, but the ones that actually capture what makes a place special. The light at golden hour, the sounds of a busy tasca, the moment you realize you have found something real.

What I do

I'm not a food agency. Not a travel agency.

© Dankamcqueen

Think of me as the friend in Lisbon who knows where to eat, and happens to be good with a camera.

I create content for spots that deserve attention but do not have the skills to shine online themselves. Music and visuals are everything: the right frame, the right track, and suddenly people stop scrolling and feel something.

Now I share hidden gems with +15k followers on social media and help restaurants tell their stories through video.

Whether you are visiting Lisbon or running a spot that needs to be seen, let us create something that connects.
