Did you know that people are checking your Instagram before they ever look at your menu?

My Process

Three simple steps

01 - Briefing

Whether you are going for a single video or a full brand shoot, we start with a quick call. I want to understand your place, your atmosphere, and what you want people to feel when they watch. From there, I build a clear plan: what we are shooting, how long we need, and what the final content will look like. No jargon, no lengthy forms. Just a real conversation.

02 - Shooting

I show up, blend in, and get to work. No big crew, no disruption to your service. Depending on your offer, we are looking at 1 hour for a focused product story, 2-3 hours to capture the full picture of who you are, or a dedicated session built around your ad campaign. Either way, every minute on set is intentional.

03 - Content Delivery

Your content arrives ready to post: optimized for Instagram and TikTok, and delivered with clear posting recommendations. For the Signature Shoot, that means 5 videos and 10 photos handed over within a week. For Targeted Ads, you also get full campaign setup, audience targeting, and ongoing performance tracking by a senior traffic manager.
